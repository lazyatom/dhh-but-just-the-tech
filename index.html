I think DHH has great taste when it comes to technology and developer experience. I am extremely grateful for Rails, and I enjoy working with it. All that said, I am very much not a fan of the direction his politics is taking. Whether or not it's possible to separate the two is a question I'm not equiped to answer, but what I do know is that while he has every right to write about whatever he likes, I also have no obligation to read everything he writes. This just makes it a little easier to achieve that.

Some people might feel that the only correct response is to fully disengage. They might be right! I welcome those kinds of conversations, but I prefer to have them in person.
